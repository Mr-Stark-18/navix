How it works

One AI system, from enquiry to delivery.

Navix is your one-stop solution, and every step hands off to the next — through our system and operations team orchestrating together.

1

Match

Our matching algorithm reads your specifications and shortlists vetted manufacturers by capability, capacity and fit — no chasing directories.

2

Order & track

Tracking Production status against defined milestones, loading, logistics, and shipping/air freight — all in one place.

3

Quality Inspection

Quality is checked against KPIs defined up front. AI flags anything off-spec before it ships, and our guarantee stands behind the result.

4

Clear & deliver

Documentation, logistics, and trade financing are managed within the same workflow — delivering goods without friction.

The platform

Everything procurement needs, made AI-native.

The capabilities we're building into Navix — each one designed to remove a manual, error-prone step from cross-border sourcing.

Supplier matching algorithm

AI ranks the right manufacturers for each requirement instead of leaving you to search, vet and shortlist on your own.

End-to-end procurement handling

One platform orchestrates sampling, purchase orders, follow-ups and handoffs — so a single order doesn't sprawl across a dozen inboxes.

Production tracking frameworks

Structured milestones make every order's status legible in real time, so delays surface early instead of at the dock.

Defined quality KPIs & guarantee

Acceptance criteria are agreed before production and checked at inspection — measurable quality you can hold us to.

Automated compliance documentation

AI assembles and checks the paperwork an order needs to cross borders, cutting the manual document chase to a review.

Logistics & financing integration

Freight and trade financing options are built into the same flow, so moving and funding an order isn't a separate scramble.
